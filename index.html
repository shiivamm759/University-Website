Course's We Offer

We offer various courses in all the fields.
Eg-BA.LLB, Bsc, B.tech CSE

BA.LLB

BA LLB (Bachelor of Arts + Bachelor of Laws) is a five-year integrated law degree combining legal studies with humanities.
subjects like sociology, political science, and history. It prepares students for careers in law, judiciary, civil services, or corporate sectors by imparting legal knowledge alongside essential social science perspectives.

Bsc

B.Sc. (Bachelor of Science) is an undergraduate degree focused on scientific and technical fields such as physics, chemistry, biology, mathematics, or computer science.
Typically completed in three years, it emphasizes theoretical knowledge, practical skills, and analytical thinking, preparing students for careers in research, teaching, and various scientific industries.

B.tech Cse

B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering (CSE) is a four-year program focused on computer programming, algorithms, data structures, artificial intelligence, and software development.
It equips students with skills in coding, problem-solving, and system design, preparing them for careers in software engineering, data science, cybersecurity, and tech innovation.
